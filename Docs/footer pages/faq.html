Getting Started
How do I create an account? +

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Get Started" button on our homepage, fill in your basic information (name, email, password), and verify your email address. Once verified, you can immediately start adding your properties and tenants. The entire process takes less than 2 minutes.

Is there a free trial available? +

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for our Professional and Enterprise plans. No credit card is required to start your trial. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period. The Starter plan is free forever with basic features.

How do I add my first property? +

After logging in, go to the Dashboard and click "Add Property". Fill in the property details including name, address, number of units, and monthly rent. You can also upload photos and documents. Once saved, you can start adding tenants to that property.

What information can I track for each tenant? +

You can track comprehensive tenant information including contact details, lease start/end dates, monthly rent amount, payment history, outstanding balances, documents (IDs, contracts), and notes. All information is securely stored and easily accessible.

Payments & Billing
How do I track tenant payments? +

RentEase provides an easy payment tracking system. When a tenant pays, simply mark the payment as received in the system. You can view payment history, outstanding balances, and generate reports. The dashboard shows you at a glance who has paid and who hasn't.

Can tenants pay directly through the platform? +

This feature is currently in development and will be available soon. For now, RentEase serves as a tracking and management tool. Tenants make payments through your preferred method (cash, bank transfer, etc.), and you record them in the system.

What payment methods do you accept for subscriptions? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and GCash. All payments are processed securely through our trusted payment partner. You'll receive email receipts for all transactions.

Will I be charged if I cancel during the trial? +

No! Your trial is completely free with no obligations. If you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be charged anything. You can cancel anytime through your account settings or by contacting support.

Features & Functionality
Can I manage multiple properties? +

Yes! All plans support multiple properties. The Starter plan allows up to 5 properties, Professional up to 20, and Enterprise offers unlimited properties. You can easily switch between properties and view consolidated reports across all locations.

Does RentEase work on mobile devices? +

Absolutely! RentEase is fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Access your rental information from anywhere using any modern web browser. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon!

Can I export my data? +

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans include data export features. You can export reports, payment histories, and tenant information in PDF and Excel formats. This is useful for accounting, tax purposes, or creating backups.

Do you offer SMS or email notifications? +

SMS and email notifications are available on Professional and Enterprise plans. You'll receive automatic notifications for important events like payment confirmations, overdue reminders, and lease expiration alerts.

Security & Privacy
Is my data safe and secure? +

Yes! We take security very seriously. All data is encrypted in transit (using SSL/TLS) and at rest. We use industry-standard security measures, perform regular backups, and store data in secure cloud servers. We never share your data with third parties.

What happens to my data if I cancel? +

Your data remains accessible until the end of your billing period. Before cancelling, you can export all your information. After 30 days of account closure, data is permanently deleted from our servers according to our privacy policy.

Do you comply with data protection regulations? +

Yes! RentEase complies with Philippine data protection laws and international best practices. We have a comprehensive privacy policy and data protection measures in place. You maintain full ownership of your data at all times.

Are regular backups performed? +

Yes! We perform automatic daily backups of all data. In the unlikely event of data loss, we can restore your information. Multiple backup copies are stored in different secure locations for maximum reliability.

Account Management
How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +

You can change your plan anytime from your account settings. Go to "Billing" and select "Change Plan". Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. We'll prorate any charges accordingly.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees or penalties. Your account will remain active until the end of your billing period. You can reactivate your account anytime by resubscribing.

How do I reset my password? +

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the link to create a new password. For security reasons, the link expires after 1 hour.

Can multiple users access the same account? +

Multi-user access is available on Enterprise plans. This allows you to grant team members or staff access to manage properties while maintaining control over permissions. Contact sales for more information about multi-user features.
