Getting Started

PetCare is an online appointment system for home-based pet check-ups. Clients can sign up or log in to book pet services, choose a date and time, and confirm their appointment — all in a few easy steps.

To begin, users must create an account or sign in. Once logged in, navigate to the “Book” tab to start scheduling a home service. You’ll be prompted to select a service (e.g., Vaccination, Grooming), input your pet’s details (type, size, breed), and choose a date and time based on availability. An invoice will be generated before final confirmation.

Admins will review the submitted request and approve or decline it. Users can check the status of their appointments on the “Transactions” tab.

User Roles & Permissions

There are two main roles in the system: Admin and Client. Each has specific permissions to ensure smooth service management.

Role Description Permissions
Admin System administrator Can view and manage all bookings, set available time slots, and approve/decline appointments
Client Pet owner or customer Can book appointments, view transaction history, and manage account details

Notifications

The system notifies users via email when their appointment is approved, declined, or updated. Admins receive alerts when a new booking is submitted. More notification options (SMS, calendar sync) may be added in future updates.

Account Settings

Clients can manage their profile, update contact information, and change their passwords under the “Account” tab. Admins can also configure service settings and available schedules through their dashboard.

FAQs

Q: Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
A: Currently, clients must contact the admin directly for cancellations or changes. This feature is planned for future versions.

Q: What services are offered?
A: Services include vaccinations, deworming, check-ups, and grooming. Available services are listed during booking.
