FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have

How quickly can I get started with RentEase?

You can get started immediately! Simply sign up for a free account, and you'll have access to our platform within minutes. Our onboarding process is straightforward and includes helpful tutorials.

Do you offer training for new users?

Yes! We provide comprehensive video tutorials, documentation, and live support. Enterprise plan users also get personalized training sessions and dedicated onboarding assistance.

Is my data secure with RentEase?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data. All information is stored securely and we comply with Philippine Data Privacy laws. Your data is backed up regularly and never shared with third parties.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll have immediate access to new features. If downgrading, changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), GCash, PayMaya, and bank transfers. Enterprise clients can also arrange for invoice-based billing.

Do you offer discounts for annual subscriptions?

Yes! Annual subscriptions come with up to 17% savings compared to monthly billing. Check our pricing page for exact amounts for each plan.
