Our Story

How RentEase Began

RentEase was born from a simple observation: many lessors in our community were struggling to manage their boarding houses using outdated methods like paper records and spreadsheets. We saw an opportunity to make their lives easier.

As BSIS students at West Visayas State University, we combined our technical skills and passion for problem-solving to create a platform specifically designed for the needs of local property owners. Our goal was to build something simple, effective, and accessible.
